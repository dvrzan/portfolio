Analyzar Privacy Policy

This policy effective December 2, 2019

What happens on your phone, stays on your phone

Analyzar does not require user to register or provide any personal information to operate the app, nor does it collect or save any data.
Analyzar uses the Twitter social network to search for tweets.
When user starts a search, Analyzar is calling Twitter standard API and the search data is not saved in any form.
Analyzar does not require user to access any user’s social network accounts to perform a search action.

Analyzar never collects your Personal Info except to communicate with you

Optionally, you may choose to provide email address and communicate with developer via email. This info is used only as you’d expect and deleted upon request.

Analyzar never sells your email address or any Personal Info you volunteer

Developer doesn’t receive payment in cash or in any kind from third parties in exchange for your Personal Info.

Analyzar retains Personal Info you volunteer for one month or less

Developer will remove your Personal Info from records within 30 days of any request to do so.

Changes to this policy

Developer may make small, inconsequential changes to this policy with or without notice to you, so you’re encouraged to review the policy from time to time. This page will show most up-to-date privacy policy, if any futher privacy policy change, the page will be updated to reflect the change.

Contact

You can contact the developer at devcontact@icloud.com if you have any questions about this policy.
